Our Mission

Prosperity that stays close to home

Too many opportunities pass our communities by. The Association exists to change that — not through speculation or promises of profit, but through education, honest trade, and infrastructure our children can learn in. Every naira of effort stays in service of the people who give it.

Education

ILIMI

We organise the repair and upkeep of local schools and run financial-literacy sessions so every member can understand money, savings, and digital tools with confidence.

Local Trade

CINIKI

We connect merchants across towns and markets, helping them find one another, trade fairly, and resolve disputes through trusted community mediation.

Community Care

AL’UMMA

From welfare support to youth programmes, we look after our own — because a strong region is built household by household, hand in hand.

How We Work

Open books, open doors

Trust is our only real currency. Here is how the Association keeps it.

Member-led governance

Decisions are made in open meetings. Regional coordinators are accountable to the members who appoint them, and any member may raise a matter for review.

Transparent records

Contributions and project spending are recorded in a shared ledger that members can inspect. What comes in, and where it goes, is never a secret.

Projects you can touch

Our output is physical and local: repaired classrooms, trained members, functioning market networks. Progress is reported openly at community gatherings.

Our Commitment

What we will always tell you plainly

The Association’s promise to every member

  • Contributions to the Association are voluntary support for community projects — they are not investments and carry no promise of financial return.
  • No member is ever pressured to give, recruit, or contribute beyond their means.
  • Questions about funds and projects are welcome at any time, from any member.
  • Disputes are resolved through fair, documented mediation — not silence.
